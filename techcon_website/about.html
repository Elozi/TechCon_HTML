History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for technology enthusiasts and has grown into a globally recognized event attracting thousands of attendees each year.

Mission

Our mission is to empower technology professionals by providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and innovation in all things tech.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in technology, including:

  • Ada Lovelace – Pioneer of computing and inspirational speaker on the power of programming.

  • Alan Turing – Father of artificial intelligence, featured in a retrospective keynote.

  • Grace Hopper – Trailblazer in computer science and advocate for women in tech.
